About Me
I am a computer science student at California State University Sacramento graduating in 2025. I have previously interned for a non-profit as a software engineer and made several contributions to an open-source project with over 17 million users. Prior to the internship, I held a leadership position in a team of seven in developing a React project for a start-up company, and I soon found myself working on multiple personal projects. As a result, I always look forward to learning about new technologies and working with familiar and new codebases!
